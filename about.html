About DrinkIQ

Why We Built This

DrinkIQ is a free, privacy-respecting BAC calculator built specifically for India. We believe responsible drinking starts with understanding your limits.

01 — The Problem

Drinking Without Data

India has a growing social drinking culture, but very few tools help people understand their own limits. Most people estimate their intoxication by feel — which is notoriously unreliable. By the time you feel drunk, your judgement is already impaired.

The result: Preventable drunk driving incidents, poor decision-making, and health consequences that could be avoided with better information.

02 — The Science

How BAC Estimation Works

DrinkIQ uses the Widmark equation — the established scientific formula for estimating Blood Alcohol Content. It's the same equation used by forensic toxicologists and researchers worldwide.

Widmark Equation
BAC = (A / (W × r)) × 100
Where: A = grams of alcohol consumed, W = body weight (kg), r = distribution ratio (0.68 for males, 0.55 for females). BAC then decreases at approximately 0.015% per hour as the liver metabolizes alcohol.

Important: This is an estimate, not a measurement. Real BAC is affected by food intake, hydration, liver health, body composition, genetics, and exact drink strength. Always treat results as rough guidance only.

03 — Our Principles

What We Stand For

1. Privacy First: All calculations happen in your browser. We don't collect or store your data. No tracking, no analytics, no surveillance.

2. India-Focused: Our drink database includes Kingfisher beer, whisky pegs, desi daru, feni, toddy and other drinks common in India — not just Western standards.

3. Evidence-Based: We use established science (Widmark equation), cite sources, and never make medical claims. We're educators, not doctors.

4. Free Forever: Responsible drinking education should be accessible to everyone. This tool will always be free. Ads fund hosting, nothing more.

5. Never Encourage Drinking: We don't glorify or promote alcohol consumption. We provide information for people who have already chosen to drink.

04 — What We're NOT

Clear Limitations

DrinkIQ is not a breathalyser, not a medical device, and not legal advice. Results are estimates only and can differ significantly from actual BAC.

Never use this tool to decide whether to drive. The only safe choice after drinking is not to drive. Arrange alternate transport — always.
