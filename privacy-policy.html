Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 February 2026 · Effective immediately · Applies to drinkiq.vercel.app

Short version: DrinkIQ does not collect or store your personal data on our servers. All calculations happen in your browser. We display Google AdSense ads which use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time.

1. Who We Are

DrinkIQ ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at drinkiq.vercel.app, a free BAC estimation and responsible drinking education tool. The service is operated from Rajasthan, India. Contact: github.com/umraorajpurohit/DrinkIQ.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process on our servers:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • Your BAC calculations or drinking history
  • Your body weight, biological sex, or any health data you enter
  • Your location or IP address (beyond what Vercel's hosting infrastructure logs)
  • Any personally identifiable information

All calculations and history are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device. Clearing your browser data removes them permanently.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

If you use the optional account feature or history tracking, the following is stored in your browser's localStorage only on your device:

  • A username you choose (not verified, can be anonymous)
  • A hashed (not reversible) version of your password
  • Your last 20 BAC calculation results
  • Your theme preference (dark/light)
  • A flag confirming you agreed to the disclaimer

None of this data is ever sent to our servers. You can delete it at any time by clicking "Clear all history" in the app, or by clearing your browser's localStorage.

DrinkIQ is intended for users aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. The tool displays an age confirmation gate on first use. If you believe a minor has used this service, please contact us via GitHub.

8. Your Rights (India — DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access information about how your data is processed
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erase your data (locally: use the Clear History button; for ad preferences: use Google's tools above)
  • Withdraw consent for personalised advertising at any time
  • Nominate a representative to exercise rights on your behalf

Since we hold no personal data on our servers, most DPDP requests will be directed to Google (for ad data) or handled by clearing your browser's localStorage.

9. Cookies Summary

  • Google AdSense cookies — for ad personalisation and measurement (third party, can be blocked)

We do not use tracking pixels, retargeting cookies, or any first-party analytics.
