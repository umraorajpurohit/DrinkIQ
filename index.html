🔬 Not a Medical Device

howbhandami uses a simplified mathematical model (Widmark equation) to produce a rough estimate only. It is NOT a breathalyser, a certified medical device, or a clinical diagnostic instrument. Real BAC is affected by food, hydration, medications, liver health, sleep, body composition, and dozens of other factors this tool cannot measure. Results may be substantially inaccurate.

🚫 Never Use This to Decide to Drive

A result of 0.00% does NOT mean you are safe or legally permitted to drive. The only safe decision after consuming any alcohol is NOT to drive. In India, the legal limit is 0.03% BAC but impairment can occur well below that threshold. This tool cannot tell you whether you are safe to drive.
